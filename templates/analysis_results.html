💡 About the Congregational Comfort Analysis Scale

Singing suitability is evaluated based on standard amateur community vocal brackets. Long, sustained notes resting near extreme boundary thresholds apply scaling penalties to prevent crowd vocal fatigue.

📋 How Options are Evaluated & Ranked

Keys are indexed by balancing Vocal Comfort against exactly how much of a pain it is to play for the band. Points are subtracted from the vocal score if a key uses difficult accidentals (like 5 sharps) or awkward fretboard/valve positions for typical worship instruments.
